Hi - I'm Kirana, I am a Data Analyst

I'm currently pursuing my Master’s degree in Computer Science at the Colorado School of Mines, building on a solid foundation from my Bachelor’s in Computer Science with a focus on data science. My academic and professional journey has been driven by a passion for exploring the frontiers of technology, with hands-on experience in machine learning and generative AI. I’ve contributed to diverse projects, from developing an interactive chatbot to deploying large language models on robotic platforms like a robot dog. Originally from Denver, Colorado, I’m also deeply connected to the outdoors. I’ve tackled 10 of Colorado’s famed fourteeners and am an enthusiastic snowboarder—firmly in the camp that snowboarders are cooler than skiers! Balancing my tech life with a full house, I’m a full-time student and a part-time “mom” to my two cats.
